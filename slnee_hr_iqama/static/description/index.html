Slnee HR: IQAMA Service

An IQAMA is a residence permit issued to those expatriates who arrive in KSA on an employment visa

To bring your family to stay in KSA permanent basis, you need to apply for the permanent family visa of your family base on your IQAMA.

Employee IQAMA request

Employee can put request to bring his/her family member in KSA.So Employee put request in system and our HR officer can validate the request.



Required Documents

Employee need to submit related document to HR Responsible Officer.Here we already mention required documents for process. After receive all document HR Officer approve request and processor in Government.



Stored IQAMA Number


HR Officer will update IQAMA number and expiry date once he/she will get IQAMA, notify to Employee.



Expiry Notification


Before 10 days of expiry system will send mail and notify to renew the IQAMA. System will change status to Need to Renewal
